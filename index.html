What is #ClimbTheWall?
Climb The Wall Project aims to help everyone in CN access internet without any online censorship. We will send Shadowsocks configuration file (one server) to our subscribers on a regular basis. Each configuration is valid for 24 hours.

Our mailer push updates on 00:00 Beijing Time.
What Are You Trying To Accomplish?
Internet censorship in CN is among the most stringent in the world. We’ll help anyone who want to Climb the Great Firewall.
Do You Make Money?
No. We're a non-profit organization. We do accept PayPal donations to maintain our servers and the email marketing system.
